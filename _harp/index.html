ABOUT

Founders and Coders is a not-for-profit community of developers and coding academy, based in London. The 16 week programme takes students through an intensive full-stack web-development course, followed by 8 weeks of hands-on projects.

This team of four students is working through the course: growing into fully-functional, real-world developers.
